    • Team Ratings Glossary

  • Conf - Conference Affiliation
  • AP Rank - Current AP Poll Ranking
  • G - Games played
  • W - Wins
  • L - Losses
  • W-L% - Win-Loss Percentage
  • Conf. W - Conference Wins
  • Conf. L - Conference Losses
  • SOS - Strength of Schedule. A rating of strength of schedule. The rating is denominated in points above/below average, where zero is average.
        Non-Division I games are excluded from the ratings.
  • O. SRS - The offensive component of the Simple Rating System (SRS), a rating that takes into account average point differential and strength
        of schedule. The rating is denominated in points above/below average, where zero is average. Non-Division I games are excluded from
        the ratings.
  • D. SRS - The defensive component of the Simple Rating System (SRS), a rating that takes into account average point differential and strength
        of schedule. The rating is denominated in points above/below average, where zero is average. Non-Division I games are excluded from
        the ratings.
  • SRS - The Simple Rating System. A rating that takes into account average point differential and strength of schedule. The rating is denominated in
        points above/below average, where zero is average. Non-Division I games are excluded from the ratings.
  • O. Rtg - Offensive Rating. An estimate of points scored (for teams) or points produced (for players) per 100 possessions.
  • D. Rtg - Defensive Rating. An estimate of points allowed (for teams) or points produced (for players) per 100 possessions.
  • N. Rtg - Net Rating. An estimate of point differential per 100 posessions.
  • Pace - An estimate of posessions per 40 minutes.
  • FTr - Free Throw Rate. Number of Free Throw attempts per Field Goal attempt.
  • FT/FGA - Free Throws made per Field Goal attempt.
  • 3PAr - 3-Point Attempt Rate. Percentage of Field Goal Attempts from 3-Point Range.
  • TRB% - Total Rebound Percentage. The percentage of available rebounds a team grabs relative to its opponent.
  • ORB% - Offensive Rebound Percentage. The percentage of available offensive rebounds a team grabs relative to its opponent.
  • AST% - Assist Percentage. The percentage of a team's field goals that are assisted by a teammate.
  • STL% - Steal Percentage. An estimate of the percentage of opponent posessions that end with a steal.
  • BLK% - Block Percentage. An estimate of the percentage of opponent two point field goal attempts blocked.
  • TOV% - Turnover Percentage. An estimate of turnovers per 100 plays.
  • eFG% - Effective Field Goal Percentage. This statistic adjusts for the fact that a 3-point field goal is worth one more point than a 2-point field goal.
  • TS% - True Shooting Percentage. A measure of shooting efficiency that takes into account 2-point field goals, 3-point field goals, and free throws.
